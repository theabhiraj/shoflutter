# Features

## Core Features

1. **Real-Time Data Synchronization**
   - Firebase Realtime Database integration for instant updates across devices
   - Multi-device support for seamless operation

2. **Inventory Management**
   - Product catalog with detailed information
   - Stock tracking and low stock alerts
   - Barcode scanning for quick product identification
   - Bulk product import/export

3. **Billing and Sales**
   - Point-of-sale functionality
   - Receipt generation
   - Multiple payment method support
   - Discount management

4. **Employee Management**
   - Staff profiles and role-based access
   - Attendance tracking
   - Salary management and calculation
   - Performance tracking

5. **Reporting and Analytics**
   - Sales reports (daily, weekly, monthly)
   - Inventory turnover analysis
   - Employee performance metrics
   - Financial summaries

6. **Customer Management**
   - Customer database
   - Purchase history tracking
   - Loyalty program support

7. **Expense Tracking**
   - Record and categorize business expenses
   - Budget planning and monitoring

8. **Multi-Platform Support**
   - Works on Android and iOS devices
   - Responsive design for various screen sizes

9. **Offline Capability**
   - Basic functionality available without internet
   - Data synchronization when connection is restored
